Common Questions

Can a solar system power my Air Conditioners?
Yes! With the right inverter capacity and lithium battery bank (like our Premium Elite plans), you can comfortably run heavy loads including Air Conditioners and pumping machines.
What is the lifespan of the system?
Solar panels typically last 25+ years. Lithium batteries can last 5-10 years, and Tubular batteries 3-5 years depending on usage and maintenance.
Do you install outside Lagos?
Yes, we have service centers in Lagos and Kaduna, and we travel nationwide for installations.
