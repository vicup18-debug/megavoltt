Common Questions

Is it dangerous?
The shock is high-voltage but low-amperage, meaning it is designed to repel and shock an intruder without killing them. It is safe when installed by professionals.
Can it work with solar?
Yes, we can install a dedicated solar panel and battery for the electric fence energizer so it remains active even without NEPA.
How long does installation take?
For a standard plot of land, installation typically takes 2 to 3 days, depending on the complexity of the wall and zoning required.
