Common Questions

What happens if the battery dies?
Smart locks usually give a low-battery warning weeks in advance. If it dies, there is usually a mechanical key backup or a USB emergency power port.
Can multiple users be added?
Yes, you can add dozens of users (family or staff) with their own unique fingerprints or PIN codes.
Do the locks support remote opening?
Yes, if connected to a WiFi bridge, you can unlock the door for a guest from anywhere using the mobile app.
