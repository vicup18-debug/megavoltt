Common Questions

How many cameras do I need?
This depends on the size of your property. A standard 4-bedroom duplex typically requires 4 to 8 cameras for full perimeter coverage.
Can I watch remotely?
Yes, all our systems come with a mobile app that allows you to view live footage from anywhere in the world.
Do the cameras record at night?
Absolutely. We use cameras with infrared and full-color night vision technology to ensure clarity in total darkness.
