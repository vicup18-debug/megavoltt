Common Questions

Does it work on cloudy days?
Yes, our solar panels are high-efficiency and can generate charge even on cloudy days. The battery backup handles the rest.
Do solar cameras have night vision?
Yes, they are equipped with infrared LEDs to see clearly at night without draining the battery excessively.
How long do batteries last?
With proper maintenance, the integrated lithium batteries typically last 3-5 years before needing replacement.
