Solar Knowledge Base

Can solar power my AC?

Yes, with our Premium Elite packages (5kVA and above), you can run Air Conditioners and pumping machines comfortably.

How long do the batteries last?

Our Lithium-Ion batteries are rated for 10+ years of daily cycling, while Tubular batteries typically last 3-5 years.

Does it work in the rainy season?

Yes, solar panels generate electricity from light, not just direct heat. You will still generate power even on cloudy days.
